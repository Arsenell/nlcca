Our Services

Person‑centred support for adults, children and families — delivered with dignity and cultural understanding.

Domiciliary Care

Daily living support, medication prompts, meal prep and companionship at home.

  • Personal care assistance
  • Medication management
  • Meal preparation
  • Companionship visits
  • Shopping assistance

Elderly Support

Tailored care plans, mobility assistance and safety checks for older adults.

  • Mobility support
  • Regular wellness checks
  • Emergency response
  • Social engagement
  • Family liaison

Children & Families

Family outreach, respite, and practical help around routines and education.

  • Respite care
  • Educational support
  • Family guidance
  • Community integration
  • Crisis intervention

Dementia Care

Kind, consistent support that reduces anxiety and preserves independence.

  • Memory care support
  • Routine maintenance
  • Behavioral management
  • Family education
  • Safety planning

Community Advocacy

Language support, signposting and liaison with local authorities and NHS.

  • Translation services
  • Benefits guidance
  • Healthcare navigation
  • Legal referrals
  • Community resources

Safeguarding & Risk

Assessments and action plans to keep people safe in their homes.

  • Home safety assessments
  • Risk management plans
  • Emergency procedures
  • Equipment provision
  • Regular monitoring
