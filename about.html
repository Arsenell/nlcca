About NLCCA

Providing quality domiciliary care and community support across North London with dignity, respect and cultural understanding.

Our Story

We are North London Community Care Agency (NLCCA), providing quality domiciliary care and community support across North London. Our team understands the diverse needs of our community and the importance of culturally sensitive care.

Our aim is to deliver safe, effective and compassionate services. We work closely with families, local authorities and the NHS to create respectful care plans that protect dignity and independence.

CQC Rated • GOOD

Our Values

Compassion

We provide care with empathy, understanding, and genuine concern for our clients' wellbeing.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in all our services through continuous training and development.

Respect

Every individual is treated with dignity, honoring their choices, privacy, and cultural background.
